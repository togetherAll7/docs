title: Tube
---

[Buffer-geometry](https://threejs.org/docs/index.html#api/en/core/BufferGeometry) short-cut for Tube that creates a tube that extrudes along a 3d curve.

```jsx
<Tube args={[path]}>
  <meshPhongMaterial attach="material" color="#f3f3f3" />
</Tube>
```
